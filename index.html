Our Solution

Food Reborn recovers food that would be wasted from business partnerships (schools, grocery stores, farmer's markets, home and commercial gardens, etc.) and repurposes it into delicious food to resell at a more affordable price to low-income communities. Additionally, we will rigorously follow all food safety regulations in order to protect our customers.

Food Sourcing

Still Packaged Food

Perfectly good food that is wasted at public schools due to excess inventory

Slightly Blemished Food

Food that is not bought at stores and markets even though it is perfectly healthy

Extra Food

Food from farmers and gardens that goes to waste because there is excess

Near Expiry Food

Safe food that stores throw out because it has been on the shelves too long or is near its expiration/best buy date

Our Mission

What we do here at FoodReborn

Food

Supplying nutritious food to poverty- stricken communities is the most important part of FoodReborn. Partnering with high wasting businesses allows us to rescue food and repurpose it into delicious meals!

Outreach

In addition to providing communities with food, FoodReborn teaches the areas we enter sustainable ways to reduce food waste as well as offers them the opportunity and skills to open a FoodReborn branch.

Education

At FoodReborn we recognize that we can not solve this problem on our own. Along with the in-person education, we have many resources linked on our website teaching how to stay healthy and reduce food waste on your own.
