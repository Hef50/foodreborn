Our Solution

Food Reborn recovers wasted food from business partnerships (schools, grocery stores, farmer's markets, home and commercial gardens, etc.) and repurposes food into delicious food to resell.

Food Sourcing

Still Packaged Food

Perfectly good food that is wasted at public schools because no one took them

Slightly Blemished Food

Food that no one buys at stores and markets even though they are perfectly healthy

Extra Food

Extra food from from farmers and gardens that goes to waste because no one has bought them

Near Expiry Food

Healthy and good food that stores throw out because they've been on the shelves too long or are near their expiration date
