Healthy Living Education

Here at Food Reborn we strive to serve healthy food, but diet is only one factor of health. It is crucial that every person understands the 3 pillars of health to be as healthy as possible!

Exercise

Staying fit with an active lifestyle and regular exercise

To exercise and stay fit, it is important to engage in a combination of cardiovascular activities, such as running or swimming, and strength training exercises like weightlifting or bodyweight workouts. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity exercise per week, complemented by two or more days of strength training. Additionally, incorporating flexibility and balance exercises can improve overall fitness and reduce the risk of injury.

Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet with all the required nutrients

Maintaining a healthy diet involves consuming a variety of nutrient-dense foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Limit the intake of processed foods, sugary drinks, and foods high in saturated and trans fats. Strive for portion control, stay hydrated, and consider consulting with a registered dietitian for personalized dietary guidance.

Sleep

Getting a consistent 7-9 hours of sleep to feel and work your best

Getting enough sleep is crucial for overall health and well-being. Aim for 7 to 9 hours of quality sleep each night, ensuring a consistent sleep schedule and creating a conducive sleep environment. Establish a bedtime routine that promotes relaxation, such as avoiding electronic devices before bed, practicing relaxation techniques like meditation or deep breathing, and keeping the bedroom dark, quiet, and at a comfortable temperature. Prioritizing sleep can improve cognitive function, mood, and physical performance while reducing the risk of chronic conditions.
